KnitClick Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Overview

KnitClick is a row counter app for circular knitting machines. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect (spoiler: almost none).

Data Collection

KnitClick does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not require an account, login, or any personal information to use the app.

Local Storage

The app saves your current row count to your device's local storage so your count is preserved between sessions. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Analytics & Tracking

KnitClick does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services. We do not track your usage, location, or any other information.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Fonts to display the logo typeface. Google's privacy policy applies to the loading of font files. No other third-party services are used.

Children's Privacy

KnitClick does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository at github.com/DianaSparrow/knitclick-mobile.
